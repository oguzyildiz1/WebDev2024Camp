Angela's Recipe for the best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • warm mik
  • teaspoons yeast
  • Granulated sugar
  • egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • cup dark brown sugar
  • tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk
  2. Melt the butter
  3. Add in the flour
  4. Kead the dough
  5. Transfer the dough
  6. After the dough
